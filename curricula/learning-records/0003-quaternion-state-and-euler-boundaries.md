# Quaternion state and Euler boundaries

The learner understands that quaternions should remain the orientation source of truth for frame conversion and logging, while Euler angles are useful at human-readable and physical-joint boundaries. They also recognize that an Euler singularity makes yaw and roll non-unique at ±90° pitch without invalidating the physical attitude or quaternion; future lessons can now treat attitude representation as established and focus on SLAM pose continuity and resets.
